About

I am a PhD scholar at the Centre for High Energy Physics, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore.

My research focuses on non-perturbative quantum field theory, lattice gauge theories, finite-temperature Quantum Chromodynamics, and large-scale numerical simulations on GPU-based computing systems.

I maintain public notes and documentation on lattice QCD, SIMULATeQCD, theoretical physics, numerical methods, and computational science.

Research Interests

  • Lattice Quantum Chromodynamics (Lattice QCD)
  • Finite-Temperature Gauge Theories
  • Spatial String Tension
  • Wilson Loops and Smearing Techniques
  • GPU Accelerated Scientific Computing
  • Numerical Methods in Quantum Field Theory
