Abstract

  1. They calculated the spatial string tension in (2+1) flavor QCD with physical strange quark mass and almost physical light quark masses using lattices with temporal extent \(N_{\tau}=4,6,8\).
  2. They then compared the results with predictions of dimensionally reduced QCD.
  3. This suggested them that; also in presence of light dynamical quarks dimensional reduction works well down to temperature 1.5T_{c}
