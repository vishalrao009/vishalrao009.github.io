Spell checking and correction

  1. :set spell – Turn on spell checking
  2. :set nospell – Turn off spell checking
  3. ]s – Jump to the next misspelled word
  4. [s – Jump to the previous misspelled word
  5. z= – Bring up the suggested replacements
  6. zg – Good word: Add the word under the cursor to the dictionary
  7. zw – Whoops! Undo and remove the word from the dictionary
